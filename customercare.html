Quick Help (FAQs)

Q: How can I cancel my ticket?
A: Log in to your account, go to “My Bookings,” and select “Cancel Ticket”.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days as per operator policy.

Q: What if I miss my bus?
A: Unfortunately, missed buses due to passenger delay are non-refundable.
