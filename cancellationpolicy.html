Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated:

We understand that travel plans may change. Below are the rules and charges for cancellations.

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure → Full refund (minus service charges).
  • Cancellations made within 12–24 hours of departure → 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 12 hours before departure → No refund.
  • No-show passengers will not be eligible for any refund.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • In case of bus cancellation by operator, passengers will receive a 100% refund.
  • Service charges and payment gateway fees are non-refundable.
  • Log in to your account and go to “My Bookings”.
  • Select the booking you want to cancel and click “Cancel Ticket”.
  • You will receive a confirmation email & SMS once the cancellation is successful.
