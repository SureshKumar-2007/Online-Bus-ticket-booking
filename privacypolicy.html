Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At MyBus, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our bus ticket booking services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email, phone number, payment details, and travel preferences. Technical data like IP address, browser, and cookies may also be collected for a better user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your bookings and payments securely.
  • To send booking confirmations and updates.
  • To provide customer support and improve services.
  • To personalize offers and promotions.

4. Sharing of Information

Your data may be shared with bus operators, payment gateways, and trusted service providers. We never sell your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We use secure encryption and strict measures to safeguard your personal data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. You may also opt out of promotional communications anytime.

7. Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
