Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Booking & Payments

  • How do I book a bus ticket?

    • Enter your starting and destination points, choose your travel dates, and click "Search Buses." Select your bus, choose seats, fill in details, and complete payment to receive your ticket.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    • We accept all major debit/credit cards, Netbanking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and mobile wallets.

  • I didn't receive my e-ticket. What should I do?

    • Check your spam/junk folder. If still missing, log in to "My Bookings" to download it or contact support.

Cancellations & Refunds

  • How do I cancel my ticket?

    • Log in to "My Bookings," select the ticket you want to cancel, and follow instructions. Cancellation rules vary by operator.

  • How long does a refund take?

    • Refunds are initiated immediately but may take 5-7 business days to reflect, depending on your bank.

Travel & Bus Information

  • What documents do I need to carry?

    • Carry a valid government-issued ID (Aadhar, Passport, Voter ID) along with your e-ticket (printed or mobile).

  • What if the bus is delayed or canceled?

    • We notify you via SMS/email of delays/cancellations. If canceled by the operator, you will receive a full refund.
