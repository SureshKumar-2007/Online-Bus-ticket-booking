About Us

Welcome to MyBus, India's trusted online bus ticket booking platform.

We started with a simple idea - to make bus travel easy, affordable, and reliable for everyone. Today, we connect millions of passengers with thousands of bus operators across the country, offering a hassle-free way to plan journeys from the comfort of home.

At MyBus, we believe travel should be:

  • Convenient - Search, compare, and book tickets in just a few clicks.
  • Affordable - Get the best fares and exclusive discounts.
  • Reliable - Choose from verified operators and well-maintained buses.
  • Safe & Secure - With real-time tracking, instant confirmations, and secure payments.

Our mission is to simplify travel and bring people closer to the destinations they love. Whether it's a daily commute, a weekend trip, or a long holiday journey, MyBus is here to make your travel smooth and stress-free.

We're not just about tickets - we're about your journey, your comfort, and your trust.
